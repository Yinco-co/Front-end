Yinco


What is Yinco? Yinco is the University of Trento application created to combat one of the biggest problems that university students are faced with every year: finding information of interest to them regarding their university career. Whether it's the CLA English exam, how to attend a language course, fees for the current year, enrollment in degree courses, it is often difficult to navigate the university website to be able to find this information. Yinco was born to remedy this: just ask him a question and Yinco will answer you. Do you want to know the deadline of the various fees? Yinco is here. Do you want to know if free movement still exists? Yinco is here. Do you want to know how to get 30 cum laude in any exam? I'm sorry, for that you have to study. From university students to university students, Yinco is this and more. Enter the world of Yinco, to make your university life much easier.
